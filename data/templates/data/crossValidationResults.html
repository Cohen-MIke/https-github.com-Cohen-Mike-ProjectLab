Results of leave one out cross validation

This plot displays the results of the cross-validation for the chosen model fitted with chosen parameters(kmers, learning-rate,epochs number, tresholdâ€¦). Each bar represents the accuracy (in %) received at the last epoch (at the end of the trainig) for a fold . The number of fold (total of 96 folds) is indicated in the x axis and the y axis shows the corresponding accuracy. The precision of the model or in other words, the number of patients who were correctly predicted among the 96 patients who made up the test-set is displayed in the window at the top. The model with the best precision after cross-validation will be considered as the best model of prediction. Once the best model has been identified, we tend to make it as precise as possible, to do so, the different simulations with modification of the parameters of the logistic regression algorithm constitute a research approach which also requires a sufficient analysis of the results obtained at each try in order to consider the correction of the parameters in the most relevant way possible.
